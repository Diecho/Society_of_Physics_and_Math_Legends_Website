About Us

Welcome to the Society of Physics and Math Legends!

We’re a group that meets to explore physics and math beyond the classroom by discussing ideas and collaborating on projects that turn into posters and publishable research. We look at a range of topics in both classical and quantum physics as well as interdisciplinary directions connecting physics with engineering, neuroscience, and other fields.
